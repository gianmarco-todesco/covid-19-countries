COVID-19 Country comparison

This chart shows the new positive cases every day.

You can add countries by using the pulldown menu on the bottom and remove countries by clicking on the small 'x' near the country name. Move the mouse over the graph to see actual numbers.

The vertical scale is logarithmic (an exponential growth looks like a straight line).

Data (updated to 2020/03/21) provided by European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control.

Source code: https://github.com/gianmarco-todesco/covid-19-countries
